About IRN

About IRN for Press

The Injustice Reform Network (IRN) is a Virginia 501(c)(3) nonprofit dedicated to combating inequities within the criminal justice system. IRN documents civil rights incidents, fights wrongful convictions, and builds accountability infrastructure for Hampton Roads communities and beyond. Founded by BeKura Waliah Shabazz and Aziza “Zee” Okoro. EIN: 41-4321283.
