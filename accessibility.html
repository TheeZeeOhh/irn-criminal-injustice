Accessibility

Accessibility Statement

Last reviewed June 2026  ·  Injustice Reform Network

1. Our Commitment

WCAG 2.1 Level AA Target

IRN is committed to ensuring digital accessibility for people with disabilities. We continuously improve the user experience for everyone and apply relevant accessibility standards across all IRN digital properties, including injusticereformnetwork.org and the Community Harm Reporting Tool (CHRT).

Our goal is conformance with the Web Content Accessibility Guidelines (WCAG) 2.1 at Level AA.

2. Measures We Take

IRN takes the following measures to ensure accessibility:

  • Semantic HTML throughout all pages and components.
  • Full keyboard navigation support on all interactive elements.
  • 44px minimum touch target sizes on all buttons and interactive controls.
  • Color contrast ratios meeting WCAG 2.1 AA standards.
  • A skip-to-main-content link on every page, accessible via keyboard focus.
  • ARIA labels on all interactive elements including buttons, links, and form controls.
  • Error states communicated by text and icon — not color alone.
  • Reduced-motion support via prefers-reduced-motion media query, applied to all Framer Motion animations.
  • Descriptive alt text on all informational images.

3. Known Limitations

While IRN works toward full WCAG 2.1 AA conformance, we are aware of the following current limitations:

  • CHRT portal integration is in progress. Some CHRT interface elements may not yet fully meet accessibility standards.
  • Some third-party embedded content (maps, social media widgets) may not fully meet accessibility standards. We are working to remediate or replace these components.

We are actively working to address all known limitations. Remediation is treated as an ongoing priority, not a one-time task.

4. Feedback & Reporting Barriers

If you experience an accessibility barrier on any IRN digital property, we want to hear from you. Please use the contact form and describe the barrier you encountered, including the page URL and the assistive technology or browser you were using.

IRN takes accessibility feedback seriously. We will respond within 5 business days and will work to remediate confirmed barriers as quickly as possible.

5. Technical Approach

IRN's site is built with the following technologies, selected in part for their accessibility characteristics:

  • Next.js with the App Router for semantic, server-rendered HTML output.
  • CSS Modules for scoped, maintainable styles that avoid specificity conflicts.
  • Framer Motion with useReducedMotion support, ensuring animations are disabled or reduced for users who prefer less motion.

We test with keyboard navigation and screen reader compatibility as part of our development process.

6. Statement Review Date

This accessibility statement was last reviewed in June 2026. It will be reviewed and updated at least annually, or whenever significant changes are made to IRN's digital properties.
