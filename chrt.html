How It Works

From Report to Change

STEP 01

You Report Anonymously

No name. No address. A decentralized identifier (DID) links your reports without identifying you. Client-side AES-256-GCM encryption means IRN cannot be compelled to produce what it does not possess.

STEP 02

IRN Documents and Acts

Your report enters the civil rights database. If it triggers the CJRRP, an attorney referral and solidarity statement follow within 72 hours of intake.

STEP 03

Data Becomes Change

Documented patterns become testimony, FOIA requests, DOJ complaints, and Virginia General Assembly evidence through IRN's JFV legislative partnership.

Technical Trust

Built for Protection

AES-256-GCM Encryption

Your report is encrypted client-side before it ever leaves your device. IRN servers receive ciphertext only.

Decentralized Identifier (DID)

A DID links your reports without identifying you. No name, email, or phone number required to submit.

Technical Incapacity

IRN cannot be compelled to produce what it does not possess. Zero-knowledge architecture is a legal protection, not a marketing claim.

Your Data, Your Choice

Your account. Your data. You control what IRN can access. IRN will never identify a reporter without explicit written consent.
