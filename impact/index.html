Programs

What We Run

Seven active programs across criminal justice, housing, healthcare, and education — all free, all confidential.

Trans Reentry Support

Dedicated reentry navigation for trans people leaving incarceration. The 72 hours post-release are highest risk — we provide immediate stabilization.

Community Bail & Release Navigation

Rapid release support reducing pretrial detention exposure for LGBTQ+ people facing misgendering, healthcare denial, and isolation.

Trauma-Informed Legal Navigation

Legal navigation that integrates trauma-informed practice, reducing case attrition for survivors and marginalized individuals in hostile courts.

LGBTQ+ Youth Diversion

Pre-arrest and pre-adjudication diversion programming. Early diversion is the highest-leverage intervention against long-term criminalization.

Fines, Costs & Garnishment Relief

Court fines trap people in poverty cycles. IRN identifies relief pathways and documents predatory fee patterns to drive legislative change.

Know Your Rights Education

Workshops covering police encounters, pretrial detention, and tenant rights — across VA, MD, NC, and DC.

CHRT Harm Reporting

Zero-knowledge encrypted reporting portal. We cannot be compelled to identify reporters — building an undeniable record of systemic abuse.

Data & Privacy

Our Transparency Commitments

We document systemic power. We protect individual people. These two things require the same foundation: radical transparency about what we collect, what we publish, and what we refuse to hand over.

01

Zero Voluntary Disclosures

IRN has never voluntarily provided identifying information to any law enforcement agency. Our CHRT architecture ensures we cannot be compelled to — we do not possess it.

02

Client-Side Encryption

All CHRT submissions are encrypted on your device using AES-256-GCM before reaching our servers. The decryption key never leaves your browser.

03

Public FOIA Tracker

Every Freedom of Information Act request we file is publicly logged here. Transparency about our accountability work is non-negotiable.

04

No Analytics Surveillance

We do not track IP addresses or use third-party analytics cookies. We cannot sell what we do not collect.
